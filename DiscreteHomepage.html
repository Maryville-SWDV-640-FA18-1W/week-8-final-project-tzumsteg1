Discrete Mathematics

This course, formerly a full-year course, in the 2018-2019 school year has changed to a single semester class. The topics in this class helps the student with problem solving, logical thinking, and persevering through problems. This course is intended for Juniors and Seniors. This class is intended for students who are interested in, but not limited to degrees in computer science and the social sciences. The content will focus on contemporary topics in mathematics including number theory, voting methods, cryptology, networking, logic, number systems, optimization, cyber security, statistics and probability
